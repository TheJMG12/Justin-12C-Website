What Makes a Great Laptop

When your always on a laptop and performing demanding activities, it is important to have a laptop that can handle that.

The key features a great laptop should have include:

  • A High-end Processor
  • At least 15-inch Screen
  • A lot of RAM Storage
  • A lot of HDD Storage)
  • A High-end Video Card

It is necessary to have a good processor which is fast enough, the best processor is the Intel Core i7 6700HQ (2.60 GHz). This processor is in most high-end laptops now, it is 2.60 GHz (gigahertz) this a clock frequency or clock speed, whichrepresents a cylce time, the higher the GHz the faster the laptop is. When having a high-end processor, it allows the laptop to complete tasks quicker and it quickens start up times.

Having a big enough sreen is important because it is the thing the user is staring at for long amounts of hours. Having a bigger screen may be more beneficial for gaming, video or photo editing, or completing tasks.

Having a enough random-access memory is a significant key to a great computer. RAM stores frequently used program instructions to increase the speed of a computer. So a sufficient amount of RAM for a demanding user is 16GB.

As a demanding user a lot of storage would be necessary to store programs or files. For a gamer, most games are 5GB at the least for a light game, while a bigger game will be at least 15 GB. A video editor or creator would also need a lot of storage to save all teh files creatd or used. So a good amount of storage would be at least 500GB or 1TB.

Having a great video card or graphics card is valuable to have, most games need a good graphics card to run. A video card generates a feed of output images to a screen. Although a laptop would have an internal card a NVIDIA GTX 970M would be a suitable card to have, whereas the most high-end card is NVIDIA GTX 1080M.

What Makes a Demanding User

Before investing a fairly large amount of money into a laptop, it must be used frequently and the user must be a demanding one. A demanding user is someone who is on a computer for long periods of time or doing tasks which require high speeds and lots of running memory.

A demanding user mainly uses a laptop for:

  • Gaming
  • Software Development
  • Sound Engineering
  • Video Editing
  • Photo Editing
